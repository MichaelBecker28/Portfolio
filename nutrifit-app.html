Project Overview

NutriFit solves the problem of tracking workouts and tracking nutrition and combines them into a user-friendly experience.

This project was created due to our own passion in health and fitness but wanted something cleaner. We wanted to create a fitness app that solved the problems with others and allowed detailed tracking

Key Features

Main Features I created

  • Feature 1: Allowing the user to manage their profile and can control what is available for their friends to look at.]
  • Feature 2: Calorie and macro tracking was done with an API from the FDA that reads into a database filled with all in-store products within the US.]
  • Feature 3: I also contributed in making this specific algorithm that essentially depending on height, weight, gender, determines caloric progress for your wanted goal.
  • Feature 4: Being able to log meals and view them!
  • Feature 5: Being able to not only track your daily progress but also have a calender view where you can check what you did when was very important. So we added that to extend off of just looking at your daily goals.
